About Us

WHO WE ARE

We are Model3d, a digital platform uniting interior designers, architects, and decorators with producers of interior design elements worldwide.

Just like other great ideas,Model3d has a background story, too. Our team of designers started developing Model3d as a solution to their biggest problem: finding the right 3D models for each project was taking too much time, energy, and financial resources. No existing platform had the solution needed, so we decided to create one for ourselves. Initially, it was an internal source of 3D models accessed only by our internal design team.

Mr. kwang shang - CEO

WHAT WE DO

We present 3D models of furniture, lighting, textures, works of art of world-famous brands, and much more in the format of max, obj and fbx. Manufacturers have the opportunity to represent and promote their products by uploading 3D models.

Model3d enables brands to do MVP testing: the possibility to test their products and gain customer validation. Providing dashboard information lets brands develop their strategy and gain a competitive edge.
